How To Play

1.) Choose a color. The fortune will count based on the number of letters used to spell the chosen color (e.g. "Green" is 5). After the count a set of four numbers will appear.
2.) Choose a number from that set. The fortune will count based on the number chosen. After the count the fortune should show a set of four numbers.
3.) Finally choose one of the four numbers and your fortune will be revealed. Choose wisely.
4.) Enjoy your fortune!
